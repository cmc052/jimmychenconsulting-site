About

Jimmy Chen Consulting provides independent advisory services at the intersection of analytics, operations management, and executive decision making. The firm focuses on problems where data is imperfect, uncertainty is high, and decisions have material financial or organizational consequences.

The practice is led by Chun‑Miin (Jimmy) Chen, Ph.D., Associate Professor of Analytics & Operations Management at Bucknell University. He holds a Ph.D. in Supply Chain and Information Systems from Penn State University and an MBA in Finance and Supply Chain Management.

Dr. Chen has published extensively in operations management, logistics, healthcare analytics, and applied simulation, and his work has been featured in major media outlets including The Philadelphia Inquirer, U.S. News & World Report, WITF, and regional business press. He has advised organizations across manufacturing, retail, healthcare, and public-sector contexts on data-driven strategy, service-level design, and operational risk.

Engagements range from short diagnostic studies to longer-term strategic advisory and training initiatives. The guiding principles are rigor, transparency, and solutions that can be implemented in real organizations rather than theoretical abstraction.
