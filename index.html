How I Work

A clear, structured process designed to support sound decision making from framing through action.

1

Clarify the decision context

Identify the decision, align on scope, and define what success looks like before analysis begins.

2

Assess data and assumptions

Review available data, assess quality and limitations, and make assumptions explicit.

3

Analyze and model selectively

Tailor analysis to the decision at hand using visualization, quantitative modeling, scenario analysis, and selective use of AI tools when they add clarity.

4

Translate insight into action

Convert findings into decision-ready options with clear tradeoffs and practical implications.

About

Jimmy Chen Consulting provides independent advisory services focused on analysis, investigation, and judgment under uncertainty. The goal is not to promise outcomes, but to help organizations make better informed decisions using the data they have.

This work is especially well suited to small and mid sized organizations that do not have in house analytics teams. Outcomes depend on data quality, context, and execution.

If clients wish, engagements can later inform teaching or academic projects, with no obligation and no additional burden.
