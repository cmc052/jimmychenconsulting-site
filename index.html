About

Jimmy Chen Consulting provides independent advisory services at the intersection of analytics, operations management, and executive decision making. The work focuses on problems where data is imperfect, uncertainty is high, and decisions carry real consequences.

The practice is led by Chun-Miin (Jimmy) Chen, Ph.D., Associate Professor of Analytics and Operations Management at Bucknell University. He combines technical modeling with practical business judgment, supporting leaders who need clarity, not buzzwords.

Engagements typically blend analysis, decision modeling, and communication support so teams can act with confidence. The guiding principles are rigor, transparency, and solutions that fit how real organizations operate.
